Competitive Mode

The entire ranking system goes from 5000 point which in the highest you can be, to 1 point which is the lowest you can be. Every game you win you gain points depending on how much of an impact you had on the game. The same thing goes if you lose a game but this time you lose points instead of gaining them. Once you get to a certain amount of points you rank up.

Bronze

This is the lowest rank in the game. To be at this rank you have to be between 1-1500. I suggest you don't shoot for this rank. If you are in this rank you are considered bad. I sugest you work on calling out plays, positioning and grouping up

Silver

This rank is the second lowest rank. It's skill rating is from 1500SR to 2000SR. This rank is for people who still don't know what team work is. My sugestion is to again just like in bronze to work on calling out the play. Fo example, if you see a reaper flanking your whole team and they are not aware, TELL THEM. That reaper is probably looking to use his ultimate. If you can stop him, you're at a huge advantage. Team comps are extreamly important as well. Just remember that having one tank is not bad, as long as you have two supports as well.

Gold

When you make it to the rank gold, it means your bad but you could still easily get out of it. Out of every rank in the game, this is the easist one. The main thing you want to focus on, is team play. You need to co-ordinate an attack with your team. You tell your team which way you guys are going in and how you are going to intiate the attack. Once you've figured that out, you need to focus a target. The first people you prioritize are the supports. They are extreamly vulnerable. Once you've killed them you clean up and hopefully win on that attack.
