Competitive Mode

The entire ranking system goes from 5000 point which in the highest you can be, to 1 point which is the lowest you can be. Every game you win you gain points depending on how much of an impact you had on the game. The same thing goes if you lose a game but this time you lose points instead of gaining them. Once you get to a certain amount of points you rank up.

Bronze


This is the lowest rank in the game. To be at this rank you have to be between 1-1500. I suggest you don't shoot for this rank. If you are in this rank you are considered bad. I sugest you work on calling out plays, positioning and grouping up
